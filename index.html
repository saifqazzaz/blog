Pioneering the Future of Technology: The Journey of Dr. Saif Sufian Qazzaz

Dr. Saif Sufian Qazzaz is a seasoned IT professional, entrepreneur, and researcher with over 18 years of comprehensive experience in information technology, business development, data science, and artificial intelligence. He is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of ITS-Amman, a premier IT and AI consulting and services firm headquartered in Amman, Jordan.

Dr. Qazzaz possesses deep expertise in machine learning, particularly in natural language processing (NLP), alongside extensive knowledge in Big Data, Blockchain, Cryptocurrency, and computer vision. Over the course of his distinguished career, he has held several senior roles across the technology sector, significantly advancing the digital transformation of numerous organizations.
